# KNOW-HOW

![](https://www.control-htp.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Scritta.png)

Control HTP is an Italian company that produce components for high-tech applications since 1981.

There are four key moments that characterize the development of our products:

* hw and sw design
* prototyping
* production
* testing, carried out with automatic and certified systems.

Control HTP addresses to different realities thanks to its production ranging from the smallest and most personalized lot, up to the lots of millions of pieces.

Thanks to its structure the company guarantees flexibility in the requests, production, modifications and delivery times of the products, making available its know-how and supporting the customer in all phases.
